Product Description

Kanye West and adidas keep the ball rolling with the Yeezy 350 V2 Carbon. This release marks the 12th colorway of the favorite 350 V2 model in 2020 and this colorway’s dark-hued upper makes it the perfect piece of footwear for fall. The Yeezy 350 V2 Carbon holds all of the familiar elements that make the silhouette so popular; breathable Primeknit solid black mesh with its signature side stripe, ribbed patterned off-white midsole, and classic BOOST cushioning make the 350 V2 Carbon easy to love and easy to wear. The Yeezy 350 V2 Carbon released in October of 2020.
